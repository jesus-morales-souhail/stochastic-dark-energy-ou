Desqueezing relaxation time — summary
=====================================
Parameters: N = 150, r = 1.5, omega = 2.4
Metric: half-life of |<a^2>|(t) (anomalous correlator)

Result:
  |<a^2>|(t) ∝ exp(-gamma t)
  t_1/2 = ln(2) / gamma
  Verified: gamma * t_1/2 = ln(2) ≈ 0.6931  (relative error < 0.05%)

n_th dependence:
  Does not change t_1/2 of |<a^2>|.
  Sets the thermal floor Var(q)_ss = n_th + 1/2.

Cosmological dictionary:
  theta  <->  gamma     (mean-reversion / restoration rate)
  n_th   <->  residual noise floor (not the half-life)
  See notes/desqueezing-relaxation-vacuum-fluctuations-note.md
